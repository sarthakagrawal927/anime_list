The Narrative

In the year 2099, the line between man and machine has dissolved into a neon-soaked haze. NIGHTWALKER follows the descent of Kael, a memory-thief operating in the lower-gut of Neo-Tokyo. When he accidentally synthesizes a memory fragment from the city's ruling AI, he becomes the most hunted ghost in the system.

A visceral exploration of identity, digital afterlife, and the price of silence in a world that never stops screaming. NIGHTWALKER breaks traditional genre boundaries with its avant-garde visual style and pulse-pounding electronic score.
